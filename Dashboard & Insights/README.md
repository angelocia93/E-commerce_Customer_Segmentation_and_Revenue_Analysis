Dashboard & Insights

This folder contains the final Power BI dashboard and the main insights derived from the analysis.

The dashboard provides a clear overview of business performance, including key KPIs such as total customers, total orders, total revenue, and average order value . It also highlights revenue trends over time, top-performing product categories, and customer segmentation.

The insights focus on interpreting these results, analyzing revenue growth, customer behavior, and segment distribution. Particular attention is given to identifying opportunities for customer retention, revenue optimization, and category performance improvement .

All visualizations and insights are designed to support data-driven decision making.
